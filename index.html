About

Two decades of building. Now building smarter.

I'm an Agentic AI Systems Consultant with two decades of enterprise IT behind me — spanning logistics, freight forwarding, customs brokerage, cloud platforms, hospitality, and state government. My professional development includes McKinsey's Management Accelerator Program and executive education at Columbia Business School.

My work sits at the intersection of API architecture, distributed systems, and intelligent automation. I design systems that don't just connect — they reason, retrieve, and respond. I've designed AI-powered knowledge systems with multi-step RAG pipelines, dual-mode chatbot personas, conflict detection with SME routing, and intelligent onboarding workflows using n8n and enterprise APIs.

Over my career I've worked across geographies — collaborating with clients at NEC Japan and Toshiba Japan early on, through to engineering teams in India, stakeholders across the US, and developers in Brazil.

My strength is technical orchestration: connecting complex systems, bridging business and engineering, and turning ambiguous requirements into architecture that holds.
